How to Get into Modern Comics

Now has never been a better time to get into comics. DC has released new books for the Absolute Universe Initiative. A new alternate universe where classic heroes are agents of chaos against a world organization ruled by evil.

Marvel is wrapping up their two year saga in the Ultimate Universe as well. A storyline of a world created and controlled by an evil Mister Fantastic

There are many different ways to get into comics these days. The most popular methods including:

Physical Single Issues

Monthly releases, the quickets physical release

Trade Paperbacks

Standard graphic novel containing one arc of 5-6 issues

Ominbus Hardcovers

Large collections of entire storylines by a writer or entire events
